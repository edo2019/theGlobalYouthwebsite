MISSION

The mission of the global youth coalition is By building capacities, creating networks, and fostering collaboration, we aim to create a powerful youth-led movement that can advocate for ambitious climate policies, secure climate finance for projects and solutions and take active participation in climate change conferences, ensuring the safety and well-being of our youth members.

VISION

Our vision is to create a world where young people are empowered, equipped and safe to lead the fight against climate change. Through our coalition, we aim to build a strong, cohesive, and effective youth-led movement that can drive advocacy for real and lasting change in the way we address the global climate crisis in the climate negotiations. We believe that by working together, we can create a future that is sustainable, equitable, and resilient for all, with the active participation and safety of our youth members in every step of the way.


Join With Us

VALUES

Inclusivity: We believe that proactive young people should have the opportunity to participate in the coalition and contribute to decision-making, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Empowerment: We believe in empowering young people to take action and lead the fight against climate change.

Collaboration: We believe in fostering collaboration and building networks among young people and organizations working on climate change.

Sustainability: We believe in promoting sustainable and equitable solutions to address the global climate crisis.

Sociocracy: We value using sociocracy as a governance structure, a decision-making method that emphasizes collaboration, consent, and transparency, to ensure that all voices are heard and decisions are made with the participation of all members.

Safety: We value the safety and well-being of our youth members, and we will make sure to prioritize their safety in every step of the way.

By adhering to these values, the global youth coalition will create a culture of inclusivity, empowerment, and collaboration that will drive meaningful change in the fight against climate change. The use of sociocracy will ensure that all members of the coalition have a say in decision-making and that the coalition's activities are transparent and accountable. The prioritization of safety will ensure that the youth members are safe and well during the conferences and other activities.
